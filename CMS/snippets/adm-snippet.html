Introduction

We are proud to introduce Government Graduate College Jauharabad as a major public sector college in district Khushab of Punjab, Pakistan. It started its humble journey in 1959. Soon it was given the status of a Degree College. Now-a-days, it offers classes from Intermediate to Masters level. The College has an ambitious agenda of quality-centric academic growth in the foreseeable future. It ultimately aims to become a hub of higher education in Pakistan.

Why Study at GGCJ?

With the blend of dynamic academic programs, we are vying to attain academic excellence through sustainable institutional development.
